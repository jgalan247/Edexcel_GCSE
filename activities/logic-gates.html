Gate Descriptions

  • AND: Output is 1 only when ALL inputs are 1
  • OR: Output is 1 when ANY input is 1
  • NOT: Inverts the input (0 becomes 1, 1 becomes 0)
  • XOR: Output is 1 when inputs are DIFFERENT
  • NAND: Opposite of AND (NOT AND)
  • NOR: Opposite of OR (NOT OR)

How to Play

Enter 0 or 1 in the "Your Answer" column to complete the truth table. The answer will turn green if correct, red if incorrect.
