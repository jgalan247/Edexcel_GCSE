Study Tips

Get the most out of these video resources

Active Learning

Do not just watch the videos passively. Take notes, pause to think about concepts, and try the examples yourself. This will help you retain the information much better.

Review and Repeat

Return to videos on topics you find challenging. Spaced repetition is one of the most effective ways to move information into long-term memory.

Combine with Practice

After watching videos on a topic, practice with related past paper questions. This helps you apply your knowledge in an exam context and identify any gaps in understanding.
