Assessment Overview

Understanding how you'll be examined

Paper 1

Principles of Computer Science

  • Duration: 1 hour 30 minutes
  • Marks: 75 marks (50%)
  • Format: Written examination
  • Topics: 1, 2, 3, 4, 5
  • Questions: Short answer, extended writing, calculations

Paper 2

Application of Computational Thinking

  • Duration: 2 hours
  • Marks: 75 marks (50%)
  • Format: On-screen programming exam
  • Topics: 6 (Problem Solving)
  • Language: Python 3
