Assessment Overview

Paper 1

Principles of Computer Science

  • Duration: 1 hour 30 minutes
  • Marks: 75 marks
  • Weight: 50% of GCSE
  • Content: Topics 1-5
  • Format: Written examination

Paper 2

Application of Computational Thinking

  • Duration: 2 hours
  • Marks: 75 marks
  • Weight: 50% of GCSE
  • Content: Topic 6
  • Format: On-screen examination (Python)
