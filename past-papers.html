Exam Tips

Paper 1 Tips

Paper 1 covers Topics 1-5 (Computational Thinking, Data, Computers, Networks, and Issues & Impact). Focus on understanding concepts and being able to explain them clearly. Many questions require you to apply your knowledge to unfamiliar scenarios.

Paper 2 Tips

Paper 2 is an on-screen examination focusing on programming in Python. Practice writing, debugging, and tracing code. Make sure you are comfortable with file handling, data structures, and common algorithms.

Time Management

Both papers have approximately 1 mark per minute. For Paper 1, spend about 1 minute per mark. For Paper 2, the 2-hour duration gives you more time for coding and testing your solutions.
